Privacy & Transparency

Kura is built on the principle of Zero-Knowledge. We believe your security shouldn't come at the cost of your privacy.

🌐

Internet Usage

Kura only accesses the internet to fetch app icons/badges from official repositories and to check for GitHub releases. This ensures your app-locker looks native and stays updated.

⚙️

User Control

Automated update checks and metadata fetching can be toggled off at any time in the App Settings. You are in full control of when Kura connects to the web.

🔑

Permissions

We request "Usage Access" and "Overlay" permissions solely to detect which app is opening and to show the lock screen. No biometric data ever leaves your device's secure enclave.

🛡️

No Tracking

Zero analytics. Zero crash reports sent to third parties. Zero advertisements. The code is 100% open-source for public audit.
